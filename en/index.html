Architectural Solutions Division

Since 2005, SYNRC’s Architectural Solutions Division has developed and maintained ERP.UNO, a robust enterprise automation system built on formal specifications. Designed for scalable, heterogeneous platforms, ERP.UNO supports applications and services through a distributed database and service bus. It is deployed in banking, messaging systems, government agencies, and various organizations across North America, Europe, and Asia.

Research & Development Center

Established in 2015, the SYNRC Research & Development Center, through its Groupoid Infinity Lab, focuses on formal languages and environments. Leveraging AXIO/1 for specifying protocols and interfaces, the center also conducts advanced research in homotopic type theory and mathematical formalization. Key activities include:

  • High-impact research and prototype development
